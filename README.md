## 📊 Supermarket Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze supermarket transaction data to uncover sales patterns, customer behavior, and operational insights.

---
## 🔧 Techniques Used
- Data Cleaning
- Data Manipulation (Filtering, Sorting, Grouping)
- Pivot Table & Crosstab
- Feature Engineering (Datetime)
- Data Scaling (StandardScaler, MinMaxScaler)

---
## 📊 Quantity by Product Line and Gender
![Product Gender](images/product_line_gender_quantity.png)

**Insight:**
Female customers tend to purchase more in most product categories such as Fashion Accessories and Food & Beverages. However, male customers dominate in Health and Beauty products.

**Business Insight:**
Understanding gender-based purchasing behavior can help businesses design targeted marketing strategies and product promotions.

---
## 📊 Revenue by Product Line and Gender (Log Scale)
![Revenue](images/product_line_gender_total.png)

**Insight:**
Revenue distribution is highly skewed, with certain categories dominating. Log scale is used to better visualize differences across all product lines.

**Business Insight:**
While some categories generate significantly higher revenue, smaller categories still contribute and should not be overlooked in business strategy.

---
## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales peak occurs during the evening
- Fashion accessories are the most purchased product category
- Cash is the most frequently used payment method
- Female customers contribute more to total sales

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
Data manipulation techniques help uncover patterns in sales behavior and support better business decision-making.
