## 📊 Supermarket Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze supermarket transaction data to uncover sales patterns, customer behavior, and operational insights.

## 🔧 Techniques Used
- Data Cleaning
- Data Manipulation (Filtering, Sorting, Grouping)
- Pivot Table & Crosstab
- Feature Engineering (Datetime)
- Data Scaling (StandardScaler, MinMaxScaler)

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales peak occurs during the evening
- Fashion accessories are the most purchased product category
- Cash is the most frequently used payment method
- Female customers contribute more to total sales

## 🚀 Conclusion
Data manipulation techniques help uncover patterns in sales behavior and support better business decision-making.
